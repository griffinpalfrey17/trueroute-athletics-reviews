Share Your Athletic Experience

Help future student-athletes make informed decisions by sharing your honest experience

Former student-athletes only
Anonymous and confidential
Takes 10-15 minutes

Only Review What You're Comfortable With

Select the areas of your athletic experience you'd like to share. You can choose as many or as few as you want.

Coaching & Player Development

How well the coaching staff supports athletes both on and off the field — including strength and position-specific development, fairness in playing decisions, and clear communication about roles and expectations.

Team Culture & Environment

What it feels like to be part of the team — including the overall vibe in the locker room, how players treat one another across classes, and the balance between a focused and social atmosphere.

Time Commitment & Training Load

How demanding the athletic schedule is during the season and offseason — including required workouts, meetings, and how much time athletes have to themselves outside of sports.

Facilities & Travel Experience

The quality and upkeep of athletic spaces — including fields, locker rooms, gyms, and film rooms — along with how comfortable and professional team travel is for games and events.

Playing Time & Opportunity

Whether athletes are given real chances to earn playing time — including early opportunities, transparency in decision-making, and how often players transfer in or out of the program.

Nutrition & Medical Resources

The level of care and support available to athletes — including access to sport-specific meals, snacks, supplements, athletic trainers, injury prevention, and rehab services.

Other Experience

Any other aspects of your athletic experience that don't fit into the categories above — academic support, financial aid, social life, or anything else you'd like to share.
