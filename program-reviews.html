Amazing program with world-class facilities

Stanford Baseball exceeded all my expectations. The coaching staff is incredibly knowledgeable and supportive, always pushing us to be our best both on and off the field. The facilities are top-notch - from the training rooms to the stadium itself. Academic support is phenomenal, which is crucial at Stanford.

The team culture is really strong. Upperclassmen do a great job mentoring younger players, and there's a real sense of brotherhood. The work-life balance can be challenging during season, but the coaching staff understands the academic demands and works with you.
