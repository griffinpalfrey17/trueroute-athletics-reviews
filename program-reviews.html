"Stanford Baseball changed my life - both as an athlete and student"

Coming to Stanford was the best decision I ever made. The coaching staff genuinely cares about your development as both a player and person. Coach Johnson worked with me individually on my mechanics, and I saw immediate improvement in my velocity and control.

The academic support is incredible. They have tutors specifically for athletes, and professors are understanding when you have travel conflicts. I graduated with a 3.7 GPA in Engineering while playing D1 baseball - something I never thought possible.

Key Highlights:
• Personalized coaching approach
• World-class facilities
• Strong academic support
• Lifelong friendships
