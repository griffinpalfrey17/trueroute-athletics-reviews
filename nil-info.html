Key Insights

Important trends and findings from our NIL data analysis

Football Dominates
Football accounts for 52% of all disclosed NIL deals, with an average deal value of $47,000. Top quarterbacks and skill position players command the highest premiums.
↗ 67% increase from last year
Basketball Rising
Men's basketball NIL deals have grown 89% year-over-year, with social media influence becoming a key factor in deal valuations.
↗ 89% increase from last year
Local Business Focus
67% of NIL deals come from local businesses near campus, emphasizing the importance of community connections for student-athletes.
↗ 23% increase from last year
Women's Sports Growth
Women's sports NIL deals have increased 156% year-over-year, led by basketball, gymnastics, and softball athletes.
↗ 156% increase from last year
